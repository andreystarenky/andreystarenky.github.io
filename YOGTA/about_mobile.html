About
YOGTA is an index of Youth Organizations located in the GTA dedicated to helping students discover more about their community and look for opportunities to develop their leadership skills.

The goal of YOGTA is to reach as many students as possible in high school and university to allow them to find an organization that meets their interests and allows them to learn more about their career paths.


Many students across the world often struggle with finding a career path that suits them, and don't always have access to tools and resources that will assist them with doing so. Thanks to many youth organizations across Ontario, students are able to learn more about the fields of Business, Medicine, IT, and Health to name a few.


One of the most difficult steps for students in finding these Organizations is knowing where to look. YOGTA helps students to learn more about various Youth Organizations by displaying them each with a clickable logo that will open a lightbox containing information about the organization, as well as well as links to their website and social medias.
