YOGTA is an index of Youth Organizations located in the GTA
dedicated to helping students discover more about their community
and look for opportunities to develop their leadership skills.


The Junior Economic Club (JEC) of Toronto is a federally registered non-profit organization operated by high school students that strives to promote learning opportunities about the world of business. Our mission focuses on exposing ambitious high school students to free events regardless of socio-economic background in order to level the playing field for students across the GTA. In the past, we have partnered with organizations like the Globe and Mail, TMX Group and Mckinsey & Company in order to explore topics ranging from emerging technologies to fundamentals of investing, and beyond.




The Federation of Canadian Secondary Students / Fédération des élèves du secondaire au Canada (FCSS-FESC) is an entirely non-partisan youth-led charity that strives to advocate for students in secondary schools and CÉGEPs across Canada. We offer programmes that enhance and enrich the educational experience while also empowering students to become engaged stakeholders in the education system. We will support students in navigating the stresses of high school life and help build leadership, resiliency, and coping skills to reduce harm and maximize outcomes in education.

Operation Med School (OMS) is a non-profit, student-run organization that hosts one-day conferences in Canada and the USA to educate youth about the journey of a medical career. Our goal is to inspire, inform and encourage students to follow their medical dreams and head bravely into their future endeavors.

TOHacks aims to connect talented youth entrepreneurs, leaders and innovators with the top industry professionals in rising fields of new technologies. Having recently hosted its inaugural hackathon, TOHacks will continue to foster an environment that promises to embolden these students towards pursuing a career in STEAM. We believe in the youth of today and know that when they are given the proper tools and guidance, they will become the leaders of tomorrow who are capable of taking on any challenges and being critical problem solvers.

Project 5K is a non-profit organization created by youths for youths. The founders of Project 5K noticed that many youths want to volunteer, but they don't know where to volunteer. At the same time, there are many events who don't have enough volunteers. That's the reason why Project 5K started back in 2013. We aim to make volunteering opportunities more easily accessible for youths and connect them to various enjoyable volunteer opportunities to make their community a better place. By signing up for Project 5K, you get volunteering opportunities directly to your email and we make the sign up process both simple and efficient for both our volunteers and our event partners.


Project Pulse is an organization that strives to familiarize motivated students with the world of health sciences. Pulse hopes to offer yearly conventions throughout Canada that seeks to enrich and enlighten youth on the field of health sciences. These conferences establish connections with brilliant individuals, a valuable network of health professionals and university representatives




Prospective Medical Professionals (PuMP) is a youth not-for-profit organization on a mission to help aspiring students understand the medical field and its careers. Founded in March of 2018, the organization started out as a group of high school students who looked to tackle problems faced by a great deal of youth who were curious about medicine – difficulty finding medical learning opportunities for secondary school students.



Y2 Entrepreneurship Labs is an organization founded by high school students that runs educational conferences for students to learn more about opportunites for entrepreneurship in the business field. We have partnered with 30 different business owners or professionals to volunteer as judges, coaches, mentors, and speakers at our events.
