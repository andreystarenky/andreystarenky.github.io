Canada Elections App

An informative app to keep track of the Canadian Federal Elections

The Application allows the user to:
- View the latest polling results
- Quickly check last 10 polls
- Explore fulls statistics of the polling history
The app was created to help Canadians keep track of the election, and explore statistics
The mobile app is currently available on the Google Play Store
